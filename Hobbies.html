Tembleque

This is a coconut based desert pudding. the ingredients you will need are:

  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 cup and 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • cinnamon for dusting
  • 6 tablespoons of warm water
  1. First, add the coconut milk, sugar, and salt to a pan and boil it. Cook it for 5 minutes while stirring.
  2. Mix the cornstarch with 6 tablespoons of warm water. mix until there are no lumps
  3. pour the mix from step 2 into the pan and cook it for 2 minutes while you mix.
  4. take the pan off of the heat and pour the mix into indevidual cups or one large dish.
  5. tap it on the counter to settle, and refriedgerate until it is set (2+ hours)
  6. dust it with the cinnamon and eat it!
